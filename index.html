Tips for good composites

  • Match your scale — frame each photo so subjects appear at roughly the same size
  • Match lighting & tone — photos with similar brightness and color temperature look most cohesive
  • Always go hi-res — these export at 2000px wide; low-quality sources will look pixelated
  • Keep backgrounds clean — simple or solid backdrops beat step-and-repeats every time
